1. Project Title

Leafcare: E-Commerce Marketing & Profitability Analysis



A data-driven turnaround strategy to optimize unit economics and flip net losses into sustainable profit.

2. Executive Summary

Analyzed a dataset of 400+ transactions for a skincare brand ("Leafcare") to identify critical leaks in the business model. Through deep-dive analysis of CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) and RTO (Return to Origin), I developed a strategy that improved net margins from -7.1% (Loss) to +21.8% (Profit).



3. Problem Statement

The initial data showed that while traffic was high, the brand was losing ₹26 on every order.



High Marketing Spend: Ad costs accounted for 39.8% of total revenue.

Logistics Friction: High RTO rates (21.8% of revenue) were draining the contribution margin.

Conversion Gap: A low conversion rate of 0.46% indicated inefficient ad targeting.

4. The "Improved Model" Strategy

I proposed a two-pillar optimization plan:



Marketing: Shifted from broad interest targeting to 1% Lookalike Audiences and Retargeting, lowering CPA from ₹146 to ₹80.

Operations: Implemented a Pre-shipment COD Verification process to reduce RTO losses from ₹80 to ₹40.
